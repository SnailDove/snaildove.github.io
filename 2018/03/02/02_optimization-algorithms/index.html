$$ \begin{align*} \text{Compute dW, db on current mini-batch : }\\ SdW &= \beta SdW + (1 - \beta)(dW)^2 \\ Sdb &= \beta Sdb + (1 - \beta)(db)^2 \\ W &:= W - \alpha \frac{dW}{\sqrt{SdW + \epsilon}} \\ b &:= b - \alpha \frac{db}{\sqrt{Sdb + \epsilon}} \end{align*} $$
